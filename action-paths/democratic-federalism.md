## Decentralized Governance Systems
Decentralized governance looks at how public decisions can be made at the most local level possible. This module maps out existing public channels, such as township boards, public advisory committees, and open municipal sessions, where residents can take independent civic action.

---

### Local Decision-Making Channels

#### Utilizing the Most Local Public Venues
*   **Decentralized Board Analysis:** AI to scan the active event context and identify the lowest tier of public authority responsible for the issue, such as a school advisory board, a soil and water conservation district, or a neighborhood zoning committee
*   **Public Access Entry Points:** Map out exactly how public rules allow residents to place items on local agendas, submit written comments, or request public data records

#### Safeguarding Local Public Input
*   **Open Meeting Protections:** Identify how state sunshine laws, open records requirements, and public notice mandates can be used to keep decision-making transparent and accessible to the whole community
